Shop name: PRINTZ Team Official

Shop title:
Modern 3D printed desk accessories, decor, gifts, and custom functional prints.

Shop announcement:
Welcome to PRINTZ Team Official. We make modern 3D printed products for desks, homes, cars, plants, gifts, and everyday organization. Message us for custom colors, sizes, names, and matching sets.

About:
PRINTZ Team Official is a small 3D printing studio focused on useful, giftable, and visually clean products. We make desk organizers, home accessories, functional car and office prints, decor pieces, and custom requests. Every item is printed, checked, and packed with attention to finish and function.

These are better launch candidates because they are practical, giftable, and easier to photograph. Avoid publishing any third-party model until commercial rights are confirmed.

## Photo Checklist

- Use original photos of the actual finished product.
- Shoot the item on a clean desk, shelf, counter, or car interior.
- Include one scale photo with a hand, pen, cup, marker, or phone.
- Include one close-up showing print finish.
- Include color option photos if variations are offered.

## Listing Checklist

- Confirm model license or use an original PRINTZ design.
- Avoid trademarked names in titles unless you have rights.
- Add size, material, color, processing time, and care notes.
- Add Etsy URL back into the product admin after publishing.
- Keep products without rights as drafts or inactive.

## Major Site Improvements To Consider Next

- Connect Supabase production env vars so admin edits persist on the live site.
- Add real PRINTZ product photos from finished prints instead of marketplace/source photos.
- Add checkout conversion tracking for Etsy outbound clicks.
- Add a product status workflow: idea, licensed, printed, photographed, listed.
- Add image alt text and product JSON-LD structured data.
- Add an email notification when visitors submit suggestions or contact forms.
